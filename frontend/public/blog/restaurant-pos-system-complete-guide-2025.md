## 🔍 What is a Restaurant POS System?

A Restaurant POS (Point of Sale) system is a comprehensive software and hardware solution that manages:

### Core Functions:
1. **Order Management** - Take orders, send to kitchen, track status
2. **Billing & Payments** - Generate bills, accept multiple payment methods
3. **Kitchen Display** - KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket) system for chefs
4. **Inventory Tracking** - Monitor stock levels, get low-stock alerts
5. **Staff Management** - Track attendance, performance, payroll
6. **Reports & Analytics** - Sales reports, profit analysis, trends
7. **Customer Management** - Track preferences, loyalty programs
8. **Multi-Location** - Manage multiple outlets from one dashboard

### Modern POS vs Traditional Cash Register:

**Traditional Cash Register:**
- ❌ Only records sales
- ❌ No inventory tracking
- ❌ Manual calculations
- ❌ Paper-based records
- ❌ No customer data
- ❌ Limited payment options

**Modern POS System:**
- ✅ Complete restaurant management
- ✅ Real-time inventory sync
- ✅ Automated calculations & GST
- ✅ Cloud-based data backup
- ✅ Customer relationship management
- ✅ UPI, cards, wallets, cash - all supported
