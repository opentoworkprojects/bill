## 🎯 Strategy #1: Optimize Your Menu for Maximum Profit (20-30% Revenue Increase)

### The Menu Engineering Matrix

**Step 1: Categorize Every Menu Item**

Calculate for each item:
- **Profit Margin** = (Selling Price - Food Cost) / Selling Price × 100
- **Popularity** = Number of orders / Total orders × 100

**The 4 Categories:**

**1. STARS ⭐ (High Profit + High Popularity)**
- Example: Butter Chicken - ₹350 (70% margin, 25% of orders)
- **Action:** Feature prominently, upsell aggressively
- **Placement:** Top-right of menu (golden triangle)
- **Marketing:** Instagram posts, combo offers

**2. PLOWHORSES 🐴 (Low Profit + High Popularity)**
- Example: Plain Naan - ₹40 (30% margin, 30% of orders)
- **Action:** Increase price by 10-15% OR reduce portion slightly
- **Strategy:** Bundle with high-margin items
- **Result:** Convert to Stars

**3. PUZZLES 🧩 (High Profit + Low Popularity)**
- Example: Seafood Platter - ₹850 (75% margin, 5% of orders)
- **Action:** Better marketing, staff recommendations
- **Strategy:** Add photos, create buzz on social media
- **Result:** Convert to Stars

**4. DOGS 🐕 (Low Profit + Low Popularity)**
- Example: Basic Salad - ₹120 (25% margin, 3% of orders)
- **Action:** REMOVE from menu immediately
- **Benefit:** Simplify kitchen, reduce waste
- **Result:** Focus on profitable items

**After Menu Engineering:**

## 🎯 Strategy #4: Reduce Food Waste (15-25% Cost Savings)

**Average Restaurant Waste:**
- 20-30% of food purchased is wasted

### Where Food Waste Happens:

**1. Kitchen Prep (25% of waste)**
- Over-peeling vegetables
- Improper portioning
- Expired ingredients
- **Solution:** Standardized recipes, FIFO system

**2. Spoilage (20% of waste)**
- Poor storage
- Over-ordering
- No inventory tracking
- **Solution:** Inventory management software

**3. Overproduction (20% of waste)**
- Making too much in advance
- No demand forecasting
- Guessing quantities
- **Solution:** Data-driven preparation

**4. Customer Plates (15% of waste)**
- Oversized portions
- Unappetizing presentation
- Wrong orders
- **Solution:** Portion control, better training

**5. Theft & Spillage (10% of waste)**
- Staff eating/taking food
- Accidents in kitchen
- No accountability
- **Solution:** Monitoring, security

**6. Other (10% of waste)**
- Damaged in delivery
- Quality issues
- Returns
- **Solution:** Better supplier management

**Week 3: Portion Control**
- Standardize all recipes
- Use measuring tools
- Create photo guides
- Monitor compliance

**Week 4: Inventory System**
- Implement POS with inventory
- Set par levels
- Get low-stock alerts
- Automate reordering
